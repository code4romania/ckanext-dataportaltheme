{{_('About The Data Portal Application')}}

{{_('The purpose of the Data Portal platform is to respond to three open data issues in Romania. First of all, the portal will open the data used in the Code for Romania projects to the general public. They will be accessible and viewed by anyone interested. Second of all, it will define data standards and protocols for datasets published by Code4Romania, which can be implemented by any institution interested in providing public data. Third of all, the platform will provide a starting point for the development of an open data Community in Romania. Through the Data Portal we will provide support (infrastructure, procedures, and know-how) to volunteers that are interested in opening and cleaning up public data in Romania.')}}
